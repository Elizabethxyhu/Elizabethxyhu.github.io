Eliza X. Y. Hu

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Jimmy H. M. Lee. Before that, I obtained my B.E. degree in 2019 from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology.

My research interests include constraint optimization and machine learning, with current focus on developing efficient frameworks to improve the solution quality of the Predict+Optimize scenarios.
